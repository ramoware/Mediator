Mediator for Recyclable Wastes

Empowering Communities, Reducing Waste, and Promoting Sustainability

Our platform connects local communities with resources to collect and recycle waste, promoting a cleaner and greener environment. We provide a commission-based model to incentivize participation and ensure sustainability.

We're building a scalable platform to support India's Swachh Bharat Abhiyan initiative, empowering communities to take ownership of waste management and creating a sustainable ecosystem for waste recycling.

Key Features:

  • Community-led waste management
  • Commission-based model
  • Partnerships with recycling companies
  • Scalable platform for nationwide expansion

How It Works

A simple 4-step process to transform waste into wealth

1

Communities Enroll

Local communities register on our platform and start collecting recyclable waste from households and businesses.

2

Resource Provision

We provide necessary resources, training, and support for efficient collection, sorting, and processing of waste.

3

Earn Commissions

Participants earn commissions based on the quantity and quality of waste collected, creating sustainable income.

4

Responsible Disposal

We partner with certified recycling companies to ensure environmentally responsible processing of collected waste.

Benefits

Creating value for communities and the environment

Community Income

Communities earn additional income through commissions, creating sustainable livelihoods while contributing to environmental conservation.

Reduced Landfill Waste

Significantly reduces the amount of waste going to landfills by diverting recyclable materials to proper processing facilities.

Environmental Sustainability

Promotes circular economy principles and supports India's commitment to sustainable development and environmental protection.

Supports Swachh Bharat

Directly contributes to India's Swachh Bharat Abhiyan initiative by promoting cleanliness and proper waste management practices.

Employment Generation

Creates job opportunities and skill development programs for community members, fostering economic growth and social empowerment.

Community Recognition

Top-performing communities receive awards and recognition, encouraging healthy competition and sustained participation in waste management efforts.
